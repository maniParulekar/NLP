    {<DT|PP\$>?<JJ>*<NN>}   # chunk determiner/possessive, adjectives and noun

    {<NN.*>+}
    {<N.*>{2,}}
    {<NN.*>+<CD>}

After consulting with Dr. slater, I confirmed that we needed to scrape the user movie reviws from IMDB website only for this homework 5, 7 and 8.

After reading homework 7 description and considering the review webpage layout, I decided to collect movie reviews on four different movies with 25 reviews per movie of genre "Animation", thus 100 reviews total.
